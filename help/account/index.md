# Account

Everything related to your account, your data ownership, and your subscription.

## Pages

- [Sign in & sign up](/account/sign-in) — email, Apple, Google.
- [Plans & billing](/account/plans) — Free, Premium, Unlimited; trial; cancellation; refunds.
- [Export your data](/account/export) — JSON or CSV, full history, anytime.
- [Delete your account](/account/delete) — permanent, no undo.
- [Security & data handling](/account/security) — how your data is stored and protected.

## Common questions

- **Forgot your password?** From the sign-in page → "Forgot password" → enter your email. Reset link expires in 1 hour.
- **Changed your mind on the plan?** Cancel anytime from Profile → Plans. Access continues until the end of the current cycle.
- **Need a refund?** See [Plans & billing](/account/plans).

## Support

For anything not covered above, file a support ticket from **Profile → Support** in the app. Most requests get a response within one business day.

Privacy and deletion requests are prioritized: send them with subject "Privacy request" or "Account deletion request" and we'll respond same-day.
