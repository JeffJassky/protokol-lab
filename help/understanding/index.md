# Understanding your data

Most trackers hide their math. Ours doesn't. Each page in this section explains what's happening behind a number you see in the app — so you can trust what you're looking at and decide whether the assumptions match your situation.

- [How stacked doses combine](/understanding/stacked-doses) — what happens when multiple compounds overlap.
- [Endogenous biomarker simulation](/understanding/endogenous-simulation) — predicting hormones and biomarkers from your logs.

- [Rolling 7-day budget](/understanding/rolling-7-day) — calorie math that handles missed days.

## When to read these

You don't have to. The app works fine without ever opening this section.

But if any of these apply, the explainers will help:

- A number on the dashboard surprised you.
- You want to know whether the AI assistant is making things up.
- You're sharing data with a doctor or coach who'll question methodology.
- You're a builder, researcher, or quantified-self regular and need to know the priors.
