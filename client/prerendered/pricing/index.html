Pricing FAQ

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Both Premium and Unlimited include a 14-day free trial. Your card is collected upfront and charged at trial end. Cancel any time before trial ends and you're not charged.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from Settings. Access continues through the end of the paid period. No hidden fees.

Can I switch between Premium and Unlimited?

Yes. Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

Does Free work with Mounjaro, Zepbound, Ozempic, Wegovy?

Yes. Free includes full dose tracking with half-life curves for all built-in GLP-1 compounds.

Do I need Premium to track compounded peptides?

Free covers the built-in GLP-1 compounds (Mounjaro, Zepbound, Ozempic, Wegovy). Custom compounds are a paid feature: Premium supports up to 3, Unlimited is unlimited.

How do you handle refunds?

Cancel before your 14-day trial ends and you pay nothing. After that, we'll refund on request within 7 days of an accidental renewal — email support.
