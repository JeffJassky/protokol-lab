What makes Protokol Lab different

Two things no other tracker in the space combines:

  • Pharmacokinetic dose modeling — Bateman-equation half-life curves for every built-in compound and any custom compound you add.
  • Rolling 7-day calorie budget — calories roll forward so low-appetite days after your shot don't read as failure.
  • 0–10 symptom severity — correlate nausea, fatigue, or injection-site reactions against your dose curve, not just a yes/no toggle.
  • Agentic AI — reads your full log, searches the web, creates custom foods, and writes entries into your day.
